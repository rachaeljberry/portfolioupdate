Graphic Design & Video Editing

Solicit materials for USGBC MA







I worked with the Executive Director to re-brand USGBC MA and enhance their digital presence. I developed a style guide for their Green Building Showcase Event , which took place September 22nd, 2016. I created simple graphics to explain the voting and registration process for the event, and carried the same branding across every platform and solicitation material. I also utilized old archived photos with motion graphics to excite potential attendee's and sponsors.

Also created simple content to be shared on social media platforms to support a more visual and engaging graphic strategy (as seen with the last image).

  • Date: Summer 2016.
  • USGBC MA
  • Category: Graphic Design, Social Media, Video Editing, Motion Graphics
