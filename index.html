Graphic Design & Video Editing

Solicit materials for USGBC MA

I worked with the Ecexcutive Director to re-brand USGBC MA and enhance their digital presence. I developed a style guide for their Green Building Showcase Event, which took place September 22nd, 2016. I created simple grpahics to explain the voting and registration process for the event, and carried the same branding through every platform and solicitation material. I also utilized old archived photos with motion graphics to excite potential atendee's and sponsrss
Also created simple content to be shared on social media platforms to support a more visual and engaging graphic strategy.
